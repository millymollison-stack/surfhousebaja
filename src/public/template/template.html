Property Details

This beautiful 4-bedroom beach house offers direct access to the famous Shipwrecks surf break. The property features an open-plan living area with stunning ocean views, a fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances, and comfortable sleeping arrangements for up to 9 guests.

Each bedroom is thoughtfully furnished with quality bedding and ventilation to keep you comfortable during your stay. The outdoor areas include multiple balconies, hammocks, and a fire pit area perfect for evening gatherings under the stars.

Activities

Surfing is the main attraction at Shipwrecks, a world-class point break suitable for intermediate to advanced surfers. The break works best during the fall and winter months when swells are most consistent.

Beyond surfing, guests can enjoy beach walks, fishing with local fishermen, whale watching (seasonal), stargazing from the fire pit, and exploring the local area.

Local Area

Located in Punta San Jacinto, a traditional fishing village on the Pacific coast of Baja California. The area retains its authentic Mexican charm while offering modern amenities for visitors.

Local restaurants serve fresh seafood caught daily by resident fishermen. The nearest store for supplies is a 10-minute drive away.

Getting There

The property is approximately a 4-hour drive south of the US-Mexico border crossing at Tijuana. From the border, take Highway 1 south through Ensenada and continue to San Jacinto.

A 4x4 vehicle is recommended as some roads near the property are unpaved. Private parking is available on-site.
