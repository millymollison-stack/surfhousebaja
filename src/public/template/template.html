More about this place ▼

Welcome to Surf House Baja — your ultimate Pacific coast escape. Located in the heart of the Baja peninsula, this modern surf retreat offers the perfect blend of comfort and adventure. Wake up to panoramic ocean views, surf world-class breaks steps from your door, and unwind under the stars by our outdoor fire pit.

Whether you're here to ride the Pacific waves, explore hidden desert trails, or simply disconnect, Surf House Baja is your basecamp for everything this magical coastline has to offer.

Getting there ▼

Drive to the village of Punta San Jacinto. From there it's a short beach drive (4x4 recommended during high tide). Exact coordinates and directions will be provided after booking confirmation.

Local area ▼

The Pacific coast at your doorstep. World-class surf breaks within walking distance. Stargazing is incredible here — no light pollution. Fishing, whale watching, and desert adventures nearby.

Local village restaurants serve fresh seafood and authentic Baja cuisine. The surrounding desert landscape is stunning at sunrise and sunset.
